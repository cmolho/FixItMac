Welcome to FixItMac! 

.

FixItMac is a mobile application that helps students report problems that they are experiencing with a printer on campus in only a few clicks. It also serves to indicate which printers are in working order and are available to use.

.

On the home page, you will see the list of printers with red and green icons reflecting the status of the printers. If the icons are red, it means the printer in that location in the process of getting fixed.  If the icon is green, it means the printer is functioning correctly. 

.

When you discover a problem with any of the printers, please click the corresponding printer to report.  This will lead you to the printer's profile page where you may select the specific problem that you are experiencing with the printer. An automatic email will be generated to ITS, and the problem will be fixed shortly!

.

With FixItMac, there are no more printer problems!

.
